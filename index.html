About Annapurna Logistics

Annapurna Logistics is a manpower and logistics service provider focused on timely delivery, transparent operations and safe material handling. Our team manages loading / unloading, documentation and regular route trips as per client requirement.

With our head office at Seriakella (Jharkhand), we support retail shops, warehouses, distributors and industrial units with reliable manpower and logistics coordination.

Our Key Services

Manpower Supply Services

  • Skilled & semi-skilled workforce supply for warehouses, logistics hubs and industrial operations.
  • Loading / unloading staff for trucks and vehicles.
  • Helpers, packers & supervisors for day-to-day operations.
  • On-site workforce management & basic reporting.

Logistics Support

  • End-to-end logistics assistance for goods movement with proper planning and documentation.
  • Route planning & scheduling as per client requirement.
  • Coordination with transporters and drivers.
  • Material tracking & basic reporting support.

Transport Coordination

  • Smooth coordination between shippers, transporters and receivers for on-time delivery.
  • Pickup & drop arrangements from client location.
  • Vehicle allocation as per load and material type (subject to availability).
  • Issue resolution & follow-up with drivers and transporters.

Workforce Management

  • Shift planning, attendance monitoring and deployment of staff.
  • Performance feedback & reporting to client.
  • Regular review of manpower requirement as per season / volume.
  • Basic safety & work-discipline guidance to workers.

Parcel & Goods Transport

  • Regular parcel & goods transport services as per client requirement (subject to confirmation).

  • Material safety, proper stacking & basic packaging support.
  • Support for documentation like LR / delivery receipt.

Custom Solutions
